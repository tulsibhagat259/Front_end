2.What are tags and attributes in HTML? 
ans:-

tags:-Tags are the building blocks of HTML.

They tell the browser what kind of content something is.

Written inside angle brackets < >.

Most come in pairs:

<h1>This is a pag</h1>


Attributes:-Attributes give extra information about a tag.

They are written inside the opening tag.

Always follow the pattern:


<img src="flower.jpg"alt="A red flower">
