16.What are the different tags to separate sections of text? 

ans:-
Paragraph Tag:-<p>hello</p>
Heading Tags:-<h1>Title</h1>
Horizontal Rule:-<p>Section 1</p>
                    <hr>
                    <p>Section 2</p>
Div Tag:-<div> 
            <h2>About Us</h2>
            <p>We are a web development company.</p>
            </div>
Sectioning Tags:-<section>
                    <h2>Services</h2>
                    <p>We offer web design and development.</p>
                    </section>
                    <aside>
                    <p>Related links and tips.</p>
                    </aside>
