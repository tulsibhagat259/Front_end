9.How can we club two or more rows or columns into a single 
row or column in an HTML table? 

ans:-

columns 

<table border="1">
  <tr>
    <th colspan="2">Name</th>
    <th>Age</th>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>John</td>
    <td>Doe</td>
    <td>25</td>
  </tr>
</table>


Rows


<table border="1">
  <tr>
    <td rowspan="2">John Doe</td>
    <td>Math</td>
    <td>90</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>Science</td>
    <td>95</td>
  </tr>
</table>
